Font Sizing with EM

The default font size here is set to 16px. Other fonts are then sized relative to this 16px

This outer div has a font-size of 16px
This div has a font-size of 20px (16px * 1.25em = 20px)
This inner div has a font-size of 25px (16px * 1.25 * 1.25 = 25px)
