Hello Canvas World

Create a simple 600px by 300px canvas area

<canvas width="600" height="300"></canvas>

The canvas size is defined in pixels. Each pixal is accessed using co-ordinates.

  • top left corner is at (0,0)
  • top right corner is at (600,0)
  • bottom left corner is at (0, 300)
  • bottom right corner is at (600,300)

The X, Y and Z have a font size of 48px. We need to take this in to account when position text on the canvas

  • To position X in the top left we need (0,48)
  • To position Y in the center we need (252,152)
  • To position Z in the bottom right we need (552, 300)
